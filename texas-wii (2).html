About Us

Who We Are

Texas Women in Investing (Texas WII) is a student organization at the University of Texas at Austin dedicated to closing the gender gap in finance and investing. We are a community of ambitious, driven women who are passionate about building financial literacy, developing investment acumen, and creating a network that lasts beyond graduation.


We believe that financial empowerment starts with education — and we're here to make that accessible, approachable, and exciting for every member.

"

Women belong in every room where financial decisions are made.

— Texas WII Mission

Our Programs

What We Do

From workshops to networking, we offer a full range of opportunities for members to grow.

Panels by Professionals

Industry panels featuring women leaders across finance, investment banking, and asset management sharing real career insights.

Stock Pitch Competitions

Members research, analyze, and pitch real investment ideas in a competitive format — building the skills that matter most in finance.

Consulting Project

Members collaborate on real consulting projects, applying financial analysis and strategic thinking to solve business challenges.

Financial Education

Structured learning paths covering personal finance, equity research, valuation, and real-world investing fundamentals.

Recruitment Process

We welcome all UT Austin students who are passionate about investing, finance, and building a stronger future for women in the industry.

When We Recruit

Recruitment takes place every fall and spring semester.

How to Apply

Students must complete an application for general membership. Our process is designed to be straightforward and welcoming.

  1. Submit Your Application Fill out our online membership application form.
  2. Attend an Info Session Join one of our informational sessions to learn about the org and meet current members.
  3. Decisions & Welcome Applicants are notified of decisions, and new members are welcomed into the community.
